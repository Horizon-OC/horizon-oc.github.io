# Integrations
<details>
  <summary>SaltyNX</summary>
  Enables viewing of Resolution and FPS in the overlay. Enables VRR support
</details>
<details>
  <summary>SUPER5 OLED</summary>
  Display underclock support for SUPER5, VRR is not supported on these displays.
  Enabled by creating retro.flag in sdmc:/config/horizon-oc/.
  This support will likely be removed in a future update due to a lack of usefullness
</details>
<details>
  <summary>sys-dock</summary>
  Support for up to 240Hz refresh rate on OLEDs
</details>
